Who We Are

PureMuga is a digital showcase for the Jorhat branch store of Assam Samabay Resham Prathisthan Ltd., a trusted cooperative organization with multiple silk stores across Assam.

Assam Samabay Resham Prathisthan Ltd. operates through its head branch at Sualkuchi, the renowned silk hub of Assam, along with branches in Jorhat, Guwahati, and Tezpur. The Jorhat store, represented through PureMuga, brings this heritage closer to customers in the region.

Our collection of authentic Assamese silk, including Muga and Pat silk, is traditionally produced in Sualkuchi and supplied to all branches. Alongside this, the store also features selected silk garments sourced from other well-known weaving regions of India, such as Banaras, offering customers a wider variety of traditional designs.

PureMuga exists solely to showcase the collection available at the Jorhat physical store. We invite visitors to experience the craftsmanship, quality, and authenticity of the garments in person at our store.

Our Collections

Celebrating Assam's rich textile heritage

Muga Silk Mekhela Chador

The golden treasure of Assam ‚Äî Muga silk is exclusive to our state and renowned for its natural golden sheen and exceptional durability.

The Mekhela Chador is the traditional two-piece attire worn by Assamese women, symbolizing grace and cultural identity. Each piece is meticulously handwoven with intricate motifs representing Assamese art and tradition.

Available at Jorhat physical store

Pat Silk Sarees

Pat silk, known for its lustrous white appearance and soft texture, is transformed into stunning sarees by skilled Assamese weavers.

These sarees feature traditional designs and vibrant borders, perfect for weddings and festive celebrations. The craftsmanship showcases centuries-old weaving techniques passed down through generations.

Available at Jorhat physical store

Traditional Assamese Wear

From elegant Eri silk shawls to symbolic Gamosas and traditional Chadors, our collection represents the diverse textile heritage of Assam.

Eri silk, known as the "fabric of peace," offers warmth and comfort, while each Gamosa carries the essence of Assamese hospitality and respect. These pieces are cultural treasures that connect us to our roots.

Available at Jorhat physical store

Note: All products are available at our physical store in Jorhat only. We do not offer online ordering or sales.
